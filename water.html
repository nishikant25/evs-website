Water Pollution

Definition

Water pollution occurs when harmful substances such as chemicals, waste, and microorganisms contaminate water bodies like rivers, lakes, and oceans, making the water unsafe for use.

Major Sources of Water Pollution

  • Industrial waste discharge
  • Sewage and domestic waste
  • Agricultural runoff (fertilizers & pesticides)
  • Oil spills
  • Plastic waste

Effects of Water Pollution

  • Waterborne diseases (cholera, typhoid)
  • Death of aquatic life
  • Reduction in drinking water availability
  • Disruption of aquatic ecosystems

Prevention and Control Measures

  • Treatment of industrial and sewage waste
  • Reducing plastic usage
  • Proper waste disposal systems
  • Public awareness and strict laws
