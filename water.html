Water Pollution

Understanding water contamination, its impacts, and solutions.

Definition

Water pollution occurs when harmful substances such as chemicals, sewage, and waste enter water bodies, making the water unsafe for human use and aquatic life.

Sources of Water Pollution

  • Industrial discharge
  • Domestic sewage
  • Agricultural runoff
  • Oil spills
  • Plastic waste

Effects of Water Pollution

  • Spread of waterborne diseases
  • Death of aquatic organisms
  • Shortage of clean drinking water
  • Damage to ecosystems
