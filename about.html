What is Environmental Studies (EVS)?

Environmental Studies (EVS) is a multidisciplinary subject that deals with the study of the environment and the relationship between humans and nature. It includes the study of natural resources, ecosystems, biodiversity, pollution, and sustainable development.

Importance of EVS

EVS helps individuals understand environmental problems and their impact on human life. It creates awareness about pollution, climate change, and the need for conservation of natural resources.

Objectives of EVS

  • To create awareness about environmental issues
  • To understand the causes and effects of pollution
  • To promote sustainable use of resources
  • To encourage responsible environmental behavior
