Air Pollution

Understanding air quality, climate impacts, and health risks.

Overview

Air pollution is caused by harmful gases, dust, and smoke released into the atmosphere, affecting human health and the environment. Climate pollution threatens our health, economy, and ecosystems. It drives temperature increases, wildfires, drought, and flooding. Exposure to air pollutants seriously compromises human health.
