Environmental Case Studies

Real world examples of environmental issues and their impacts.

Bhopal Gas Tragedy (1984)

A toxic gas leak from Union Carbide factory in Bhopal caused thousands of deaths and long-term health issues.

Ganga River Pollution

The River Ganga is polluted due to sewage and industrial waste. Government programs aim to restore its purity.

Delhi Air Pollution

High vehicle emissions and crop burning result in hazardous air quality. Measures include traffic restrictions and EV promotion.

Urban Noise Pollution

Excessive traffic and construction noise affect mental health in cities. Noise regulations help reduce exposure.
