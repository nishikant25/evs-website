Environmental Pollution

Understanding the types, causes, effects, and solutions to pollution.

What is Pollution?

Pollution refers to the introduction of harmful substances into the environment which causes damage to air, water, soil, and living organisms.

Types of Pollution

  • Air Pollution
  • Water Pollution
  • Soil Pollution
  • Noise Pollution

Causes of Pollution

  • Industrial waste
  • Vehicle emissions
  • Plastic waste
  • Burning of fossil fuels

Effects of Pollution

  • Climate change
  • Health problems
  • Loss of biodiversity
  • Environmental degradation

Prevention Measures

  • Using renewable energy
  • Recycling and waste management
  • Planting trees
  • Environmental awareness
