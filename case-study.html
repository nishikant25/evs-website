Environmental Case Studies

1. Bhopal Gas Tragedy (India)

In 1984, a gas leak at the Union Carbide plant in Bhopal released toxic methyl isocyanate gas, killing thousands and causing long-term health problems. It is considered one of the world’s worst industrial disasters.

2. Ganga River Pollution

The River Ganga faces severe pollution due to industrial waste, sewage discharge, and religious activities. Government initiatives like "Namami Gange" aim to clean and protect the river.

3. Delhi Air Pollution

Delhi experiences hazardous air quality due to vehicle emissions, industrial pollution, and crop residue burning. Measures include odd-even traffic rules and promotion of electric vehicles.

4. Noise Pollution in Urban Cities

Urban areas face constant noise from traffic, construction, and public events. Noise regulations and public awareness campaigns help reduce its impact.
