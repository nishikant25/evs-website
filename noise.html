Noise Pollution

Definition

Noise pollution is the presence of excessive or unwanted sound that causes discomfort and harm to humans and animals.

Sources of Noise Pollution

  • Road traffic and honking
  • Industrial machines
  • Construction work
  • Loudspeakers and music systems

Effects of Noise Pollution

  • Hearing loss
  • Stress and anxiety
  • Sleep disturbance
  • Reduced concentration

Control Measures

  • Soundproofing buildings
  • Using silencers
  • Noise regulations
  • Public awareness
