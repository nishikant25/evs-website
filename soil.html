Soil Pollution

Understanding the causes, effects, and solutions to protect our environment.

Definition

Soil pollution refers to the contamination of soil with harmful chemicals, waste materials, and pollutants which reduce soil fertility and harm living organisms.

Major Causes

  • Excessive use of pesticides and fertilizers
  • Industrial waste disposal
  • Plastic and solid waste
  • Oil spills and chemical leakage

Effects of Soil Pollution

  • Reduced crop productivity
  • Loss of soil fertility
  • Health issues due to contaminated food
  • Groundwater contamination

Prevention and Control

  • Organic farming practices
  • Proper waste management
  • Recycling and reuse of materials
  • Limiting chemical fertilizers
